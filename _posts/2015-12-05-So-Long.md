## Hello, it's me...again.
There are so many things happended during the period I am
away which definitely deserve a post.
But, you know, life can be really messy in school.

To start with, I believe it worth a mention the purpose of this blog.
I want to achieve the following objectives through this site:

* a practical way to improve my English writing skills.
* a place to share my thoughts on life with whoever reading this and
to keep a memorial to the journey of my life.

I am not that kind of guy with a enthusiasm in writing, but I do think I am an
_emotional_ one.
If you ask me how decisions should be made regard to a specific situation,
most of the time, I will look at things from the perspective of
emotion or feelings.

And I do wish one day I can absorb info and express myself freely in
English. The reason behind it is simple. English *facilitates and
improves* not only my professional study, but also my life.
I can gain access to better resouces to have *depper insights* towards
my academic field and the world.

## Accepting new things or guard existing ones.
Looking at my study process retrospectively, I always feel excited
seeing something awesome and new. I will start to explore these new
concepts immediately.
However, there is a fatal factor I fail to foresee, which is the
disruption to my current learning plan.

> If you can't focus long enough to master a skill to a minimum level,
> you are wasting your time.

You should realize that the number of things that you can take care
of simutaneously is limited. Every time you take in a new one, you will
lose an old one to some extend. You have to assess the trade-off.

> Do not make decisions blindly.

> Time is your biggest property, so invest it wisely.

## about teachers
Every semester I will obtain something to learn from teachers I have
meet. Most teachers are really kind people.
On one day of the first few weeks this semester, my elective course teacher
invited me to dinner. He encouraged me to be more ambitious towards my
graduate study. He told me to be more active when intereacting
with others, which is kind of a problem of me, one I am willing to
improve gradually.

There are some other teachers I meet whose focus mainly fall on scientific
research. I feel they are really skilled. I do appreciate them helping me
enter the field of academic initially. Hope I will be doing well
during the next two months.
