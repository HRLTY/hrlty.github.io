* > ###Occasionally, a life of fulfillment may come at the expense of a life of comfort.
  
  ​	The fact is, in order to make something you must give something up.
  
  ​	How bad do you truly want to achieve your goals? If you had to, how much comfort would you willing to give up to achieve those goals?

* ### As tech literacy is quickly becoming a must.

I totally agree that everyone should have some basic programming common sense. 

​	With that being said, I strongly believe as a professional software engeneer, we must stay keen to explore various advanced technique and state-of-the-art design skills. If you just feel content with preliminary or intermediate skill level, you will soon be eliminated by the growing tech crowd.

* ## The last thing… MOVE! DON'T STOP!
  
  Every now and then when I have spare time (sometimes even when I don't), I start to do some random things that's totally irrelevant or unimportant. That has become my way of avoiding pressure. How ironic, it's the same thing that throw me into great pressure once deadlines are approaching. 
  
  What I need is a steady and progressive way of learning. A sustainable development style of life.  It is a kind of wisdom, I must admit.
  
  On that account, don't give yourself such a relaxing time. It causes much greater pressure when something is due.
  
  >  Schedule every part of your time including your leisure time.  
  > 
  > There is no such thing as free time.
